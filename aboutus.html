    About Feed Hyderabad

    Feed Hyderabad is a not-for-profit social organization which aims to solve the problem of hunger, malnutrition and food waste in the country. We channelize extra nutritious food from individuals, weddings, restaurants, corporate offices to the people who really need it and have no means or access food.
